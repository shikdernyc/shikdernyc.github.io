About me

I am currently a first year in college, majoring in Computer Science. I have enough credit to be a sophomore so I don’t know if I am a sophomore or a freshmen. I’ve always loved learning about Computer Science and college has only enforced that even more. Outside of school, I enjoy playing basketball, lifting and recently billiards. I also enjoy working on personal projects.

For the next three to four years, I hope to gain as much experience and exposure to the field of computer science and one day become an amazing software engineer.
